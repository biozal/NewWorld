# Atlanta

## Regions

Atlanta is a large city broken up into regions of the city.  The city is surrounded by a large wall that goes around the city called the `Wall`.  This was to keep raiders from destroying the city.     

### Inside the Wall

#### Brookhaven
- Ruler

- Shops
